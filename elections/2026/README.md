# 2026 LLVM Community Area Team Elections

The Clang Area Team elections are about to open. The election schedule is:

* January 12, 2026 - Area Team nominations opens
* January 25, 2026 - Area Team nominations close
* January 26, 2026 - Voting opens
* February 9, 2026 - Voting closes
* February 10, 2026 - Results announced on Discourse

## Background

The 2026 LLVM Area Team elections are being conducted in accordance to the [LLVM
Project
Governance](https://github.com/llvm/llvm-www/blob/main/proposals/LP0004-project-governance.md).
Elections will be held for the LLVM, Clang, MLIR and Infrastructure Area
teams which are currently operating.

## Eligibility

All individuals with commit access to the LLVM monorepo are eligible to run for
any _one_ Area Team.

All individuals with commit access to the LLVM monorepo are eligible to vote in
_all_ Area Team elections.

## Nomination Process

Any individual with commit access to LLVM may nominate themselves for any _one_
Area Team election in a given election cycle. They may do so by completing the
[nomination form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLScl4V4g1uYgCPLVs99P0kmG-H-ss8N2zE00zRcDqJnGDRwRJg/viewform?usp=dialog).

The nomination form has required fields for your GitHub ID, your full name, and
a preferred email address for communication as well as an optional field for a
brief candidate statement. All candidates are _strongly encouraged_ to provide a
candidate statement which will be displayed on the election webpage for voters
to read when voting.
