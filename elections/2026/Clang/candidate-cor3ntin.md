Corentin Jabot has been contributing to LLVM since 2020.
He works for Bloomberg working on C++ conformance and Contracts in Clang, focusing notably on C++20/23/26 support and on defect reports.
He is one of the Clang maintainers for the lexer and for lambdas; He enjoys working within the Clang community and strives to be reactive on code reviews, discord and discourse.
Corentin is also active in the C++ committee (for which he has written a few proposals), and, occasionally, the C committee.
