Florian currently works at Apple, where he focuses on improving LLVM. He has been contributing to the LLVM project since 2016 and has worked across a wide range of optimizations. He is currently a maintainer of the Loop Vectorizer, where he is actively involved both in developing new features and improvements, and in reviewing contributions. His work and reviews frequently extend into related areas such as Scalar Evolution, alias analysis, and other core parts of the optimization infrastructure.
