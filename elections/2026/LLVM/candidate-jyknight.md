James is an engineer on Google's LLVM toolchain team, and has been an active member of the LLVM community since 2015. He has worked on a variety of different areas of LLVM and Clang through the years, including the SPARC backend, improved support and specification of atomics, and more. He is currently contributing to the LLVM Formal specification working group.
